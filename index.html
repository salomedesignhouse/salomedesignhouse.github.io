About

We love podcasts

Salome Design House is a unique branding and design experience that caters to podcast artists looking to sharpen their visual identity. We know that your voice deserves assets that match the quality of your show, so let us take the verbal to visual and worry about the devil in the details.

We listen to creators

We care about your input and who you are. We know that articulating your voice into tangible, visual assets is the next step to building your empire. So, we are dedicated to giving your voice the visuals it needs when it reaches your listeners.

We know design

Salomé Design house was co-founded by two creatives, and long-time friends with intersecting skills and interests. Our team has the knowledge to help your podcast grow in its visual and online presence by elevating your visual identity to get you the notoriety you deserve.
